About Konstantinos Norgias

Since I was a kid, I’ve been fascinated by transformers, legos, and computers.

I’ve spent countless hours building, designing, and rearranging physical stuff as well as designing, coding, testing, and playing with digital content such as mobile apps, websites, and video games.

What excites me is new tech, a good story, aesthetic & intuitive design, and creating stuff that starts off as a simple idea.

The digital space is the perfect playground for my interests and aptitudes.

I have spent multiple professional years coding mobile and web apps, and design won me over once I hopped on a design system project, where I’ve had the opportunity to work with multiple disciplines.

The 2 sides of the design system coin - design & code - intrigued me and I jumped on the chance to explore the full spectrum of product development, from ideation, to design, to code and testing.

I’m either fiddling with designs on Figma, coding some of those designs to bring them to life, or writing about my latest explorations on Twitter & Medium.

Currently focused on UX & UI Design, so I can be found reading, writing, and working on that.

But healthy mind in healthy body, so I can also be found camping and hiking somewhere in Europe’s hilly sides. I’ve found there’s nothing like getting in touch with nature, a breath of fresh air and a good view for reinvigorating the spirit.
